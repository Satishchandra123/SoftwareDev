MY MSIT JOURNEY

About me:

Hello Everyone..! My name is Ch.Satish Chandra. I am pursuing my Masters in Information Technology(MSIT) at IIIT Hyderabad. I have done my under graduation(Btech) in Biotechnology at GITAM University Visakhapatnam. My passion towards software field made me to choose MSIT as a platform where I can enhance my skills. I am very happy that I have took a right decision by joining MSIT. The work culture in MSIT is no way different from Corporate work life. Throughout my Journey in MSIT there are several things which helped me to do my best.

They are:

  • Learning by doing methodology
  • Continuous mentorship and guidance by the mentors.
  • Working on different real time projects.
  • Getting hands on experience on different programming languages like:
    1. JAVA
    2. Python etc;
  • Softskills which helped me to learn corporate working culture.
